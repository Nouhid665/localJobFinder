Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a worker for my specific needs?

Simply navigate to the "Find Workers" section, select the type of service you need, and browse through available local professionals.

Are the workers verified and reliable?

Yes, all workers on our platform go through a vetting process to ensure they are qualified and reliable for their respective services.

How do I contact a worker after finding them?

Once you find a worker that suits your needs, you can contact them through the contact details provided on their profile or send a booking request directly through our platform.

What if I need a service that's not listed?

If the service you need is not listed, feel free to contact us, and we will do our best to connect you with the right professional.
